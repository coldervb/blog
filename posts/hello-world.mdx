This is the first post on this blog.

I built this with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) and MDX. Posts live as `.mdx` files in a `/posts` folder — no database, no CMS, just files.

## Why a blog?

Writing forces clarity. When I can't explain something in plain language, I usually don't understand it as well as I thought.

## What to expect

- Notes on software and systems
- Things I learned the hard way
- Occasional thoughts on design

Short posts, infrequent but deliberate.
